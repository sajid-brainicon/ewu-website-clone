About East West University

Founded in 1996, East West University is a premier private university in Bangladesh dedicated to providing high-quality education and fostering research. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs through its eight departments.

Our History

East West University started with only a handful of students and has grown into a vibrant community of 12,000+ students and 500+ faculty members. The campus is located in the heart of Dhaka, offering modern facilities and a dynamic learning environment.

Mission

To produce competent graduates with ethical values, capable of leading and innovating in a global context.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in education, research, and community engagement in South Asia.

Campus

The permanent campus at Aftabnagar features state-of-the-art laboratories, a central library, sports facilities, and student lounges.
